In this experiment, a series of repeated numbers will be displayed, and you must say the number that is being repeatedly displayed. For example, if "5 5 5" is shown, you must say "5". As soon as the numbers appear on your screen, read it as fast as you can and say the repeated number, and then click "Next" to go to the next set.

20 sets of numbers will be shown, and after you finish the last one, the total time it took you to go through all of the numbers will be shown.

If you would like to continue with the experiment, press "Start" to begin.
